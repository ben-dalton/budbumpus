About

Located in Graham, Texas, Bud Bumpus has been working with leather since 1985. He has refined his craft creating beautiful, well-made saddles, chaps, chinks and more. His passion for quality leatherwork was born out of necessity. Bud had a need for a saddle and gear, but lacked the money to purchase it. In characteristic fashion Bud learned how to do it himself and found a new love.
