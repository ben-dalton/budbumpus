About

Bud Bumpus has been making saddles over 25 years. He studied at Texas State Technical College under Eddie Collie, Elbert Foster and Jean Cates. Bud opened his first shop in Paducah, Texas in November of 1986 and opened in Graham in 2006.

Bud is also an ordained minister, barber and currently studying in the police academy.
